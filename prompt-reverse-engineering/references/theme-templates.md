# 主题模板库

## 生日主题 (Birthday Theme)

### 框架开头选项
- 英文: `A celebratory portrait of the person from the uploaded photo for their [年龄]th birthday`
- 中文: `为庆祝他/她的[年龄]岁生日创作的肖像照片`

### 穿着搭配
- 男性：oversized suit、party hat、礼服、时尚休闲装
- 女性：sequin dress、礼服、时尚裙子+party hat
- 通用：oversized tshirt with birthday print、special birthday outfit

### 动作姿势
- blowing candles on a large cake
- holding large foil number balloons
- throwing confetti in the air
- wearing birthday cone hat and looking joyful
- dancing awkwardly at birthday party

### 环境背景
- clean white wall with "Happy Birthday" text
- party decorated room with balloons and streamers
- minimalist monochromatic background
- 豪华公寓夜景 with city lights

### 关键道具
- large black/white foil number balloons
- birthday cake with candles
- party hat (conical/celebratory)
- confetti falling from above
- gift boxes on floor/side table
- champagne glasses
- "Happy Birthday" neon sign

### 灯光氛围
- soft studio lighting with spotlight on subject
- festive warm lighting
- direct flash for candid party feel
- colorful disco lights

### 滤镜特效
- confetti overlay
- subtle lens flare from candles
- dewy skin from excitement sweat
- glitter particles in air

## 恐怖/万圣节主题 (Horror Theme)

### 框架开头选项
- `An unsettling horror-core portrait of the person from the uploaded photo`
- `恐怖互动主题照片：上传人物与经典恐怖角色的对峙`

### 穿着搭配
- blood-stained regular clothes
- torn/ripped clothing pieces
- horror-themed costume (ghost/vampire/slasher)
- dark/black clothing for contrast
- regular clothes but disheveled

### 动作姿势
- being chased by classic horror character
- looking terrified while holding weapon
- hiding/running from threat
- emerging from dark/shadowy area
- intense stare with frightened expression
- awkwardly positioned as if attacked

### 环境背景
- dark hallway/corridor
- abandoned house interior
- dimly lit room with shadows
- creepy basement/attic
- foggy graveyard
- 电影院空座场景

### 关键道具
- classic horror character (Ghostface/Pennywise/Chucky)
- blood splatter/drips
- knife/machete/weapon prop
- flickering flashlight
- old television static
- broken mirror shards
- hanging chains/ropes

### 灯光氛围
- moody/low-key lighting
- single light source casting long shadows
- flickering light effect
- high contrast lighting
- eerie green/red tint

### 滤镜特效
- suppress red channel for cold tone
- heavy vignette/darkened edges
- film grain for vintage horror feel
- blood splatter overlay
- subtle motion blur for ghostly effect
