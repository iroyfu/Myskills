  - Large foil number balloons (black/white/silver)
  - Heart-shaped foil balloons
  - Regular round balloons in clusters
  - Helium balloons floating overhead

- **庆祝元素**：
  - Confetti falling/flying/exploding
  - Streamers/ribbons hanging down
  - Party poppers/sparklers
  - Bunting/garland decorations

- **生日专用**：
  - Birthday cake with lit candles
  - Cone-shaped party hat
  - Number-shaped candles
  - "Happy Birthday" banner/sign
  - Gift boxes/presents

- **时尚/潮流元素**：
  - Football helmet (various colors)
  - Balaclava face coverings
  - Designer sunglasses
  - Statement jewelry
  - Unusual headgear

- **恐怖元素**：
  - Ghostface mask/character
  - Pennywise the clown

  - Blood splatter/drips

- **情侣元素**：
  - "I LOVE YOU" neon sign

- **纯色背景**：
  - Vibrant red wall

  - Clean white wall
  - Pitch black void
  - Monochromatic color

- **室内场景**：
  - Nightclub dance floor
  - Empty cinema/theater
  - Luxury apartment
  - Studio setting
  - Photobooth

- **特殊布景**：
  - Rose wall installation
  - Neon light tunnel
  - Geometric backdrop
  - Fog-filled room
  - Mirror room effect

### 3. 灯光效果 (Lighting Effects)
- **闪光灯类**：
  - Direct flash (harsh/edgy)
  - Soft flash (diffused)
  - Rear curtain flash
  - Ring flash effect
  - On-camera flash look

- **氛围光源**：
  - Neon lighting (cyan+magenta)
  - Disco/strobe lights
  - Cinematic rim light
  - Spotlights/halo effect
  - God rays/volumetric lighting

- **风格化灯光**：
  - Moody/low-key lighting
  - High contrast lighting
  - Warm golden hour glow
  - Cool blue tones
  - Color-graded lighting

### 4. 特效滤镜 (Filter Effects)
- **皮肤特效**：
  - Dewy skin with water droplets
  - Wet look/glistening sweat
  - Oil-slick sheen
  - Glowing skin
  - Sweat beads on face

- **画面特效**：
  - Cyan/blue hazy overlay
  - Red channel suppression
  - Film grain/noise
  - Vignette/dark edges
  - Light leak effects

- **动态特效**：
  - Motion blur (background)
  - Glitter particles in air
  - Lens flare/sun flare
  - Rain/snow/weather effects
  - Smoke/fog overlay

## 五、穿着描述模式

### 反差升级模式
```
[日常描述] → [目标风格强烈反差]
```

**示例组合：**
- Casual everyday clothes → Sparkling sequin gown for luxury gala
- Simple t-shirt and jeans → Full leather tactical outfit with gear
- Office work attire → Glittering party dress with metallic accents
- Plain home clothes → Avant-garde high-fashion ensemble

### 风格具体化
**避免模糊词汇，使用具体描述：**
- ❌ "Nice dress" → ✅ "Red silk slip dress with thigh-high slit"
- ❌ "Cool outfit" → ✅ "Black leather biker jacket with studs and chains"
- ❌ "Party clothes" → ✅ "Silver sequin mini dress with mesh panels"
